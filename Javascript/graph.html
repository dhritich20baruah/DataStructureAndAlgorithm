Graph

Graph is a non linear data structure where data is stored in the form of nodes or vertices and connected by edges.

    Types of nodes
  • Directed: direction of traversing is given.
  • Undirected: direction of traversing is not given.
  • Null graph: Only one node and no graph.
  • Disconnected graphs or bidirectional graphs.
  • Self loop graph.

Graphs are represented in two ways:
1. Adjacency graph and 2. Adjacency list
